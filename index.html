About Us

SCO Community Gathering

Our Journey at University of Venda

Founded in 1995, the Student Christian Organisation (SCO) at the University of Venda has been a beacon of faith and community for over 25 years. Our organization was established with the mission to create a supportive Christian community for students navigating university life.

We are committed to nurturing spiritual growth through worship, Bible study, and fellowship. SCO provides a welcoming environment where students can explore their faith, build meaningful relationships, and develop leadership skills.

Our diverse membership includes students from various denominations and backgrounds, united by our shared faith in Jesus Christ. We believe in creating a space where everyone feels valued, supported, and encouraged to grow in their relationship with God.

Our Services

Sunday Worship

Join us every Sunday at 10:00 AM for uplifting worship services in the campus chapel. Our services include contemporary worship, biblical teaching, and communion.

Bible Study

Weekly Bible study groups meet every Wednesday at 6:00 PM. We explore scripture in small groups, fostering deep discussion and personal spiritual growth.

Prayer Meetings

Our prayer meetings occur every Friday at 5:30 PM. This intimate time of intercession and personal prayer strengthens our community and individual faith journeys.

Fellowship Events

Regular fellowship events include social gatherings, retreats, and community service projects that build relationships and serve our campus community.
